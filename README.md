# Employee Handbook & Company Policy Generator

Stop paying lawyers $3,000 for a handbook your employees won't read anyway. This skill generates complete, customized company policies in minutes — code of conduct to termination procedures.

## The Problem

Every business with employees needs a handbook. Most small business owners either:
- Skip it entirely (liability nightmare)
- Paste generic templates from the internet (doesn't reflect their culture)
- Pay a lawyer $2,000–$5,000 (overkill for a 10-person startup)

## The Solution

Four specialized prompts that build your complete handbook section by section — customized to your company, state, industry, and culture.

## What You Get

### Prompt 1 — Core Policies (FREE)
The essential four:
- **Code of Conduct** — professional standards, communication norms, conflict of interest
- **Attendance & Hours** — work hours, tardiness, call-out procedures, remote/hybrid rules
- **PTO & Vacation** — accrual or unlimited, rollover, blackout dates, requesting time off
- **Remote Work Policy** — eligibility, home office requirements, availability expectations, expense policy

### Prompt 2 — Compensation & Benefits
- Pay schedule and direct deposit policy
- Overtime rules (FLSA-aware)
- Expense reimbursement process and limits
- Benefits enrollment summary (health, dental, vision, 401k)
- Bonus and commission policy framework

### Prompt 3 — Performance & Discipline
- Progressive discipline policy (verbal → written → PIP → termination)
- Performance review schedule and process
- Termination procedures (voluntary and involuntary)
- References and background check policy
- Manager guidance notes for each step

### Prompt 4 — Legal & Compliance
- Anti-harassment and anti-discrimination policy (EEOC-aligned)
- ADA/reasonable accommodation procedure
- At-will employment statement
- Confidentiality and NDA acknowledgment
- Arbitration clause (optional, with toggle)
- Social media policy
- Employee acknowledgment signature page
