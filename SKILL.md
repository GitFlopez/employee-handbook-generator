## What This Skill Does

Generates a complete, legally-aware employee handbook and company policy suite for small businesses (1–200 employees). Covers everything from onboarding policies to termination procedures — documents that lawyers charge $2,000–$5,000 to produce.

**Four prompts covering the complete handbook:**

1. **Core Policies (FREE)** — Code of conduct, attendance, PTO/vacation, remote work policy
2. **Compensation & Benefits** — Pay schedule, overtime, expense reimbursement, benefits summary
3. **Performance & Discipline** — Progressive discipline policy, performance improvement framework, termination procedures
4. **Legal & Compliance** — Anti-harassment, anti-discrimination, ADA accommodations, at-will employment, arbitration clause

## Inputs Required

- Company name, industry, state/location
- Employee count and structure (remote/hybrid/in-person)
- Key policies you want to include or customize
- Tone (formal corporate vs. friendly startup)

## Outputs

- Full policy text, ready to paste into a Google Doc or Word template
- Manager guidance notes (how to enforce each policy)
- Employee acknowledgment signature page
