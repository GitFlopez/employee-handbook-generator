# Marketing Strategy — Employee Handbook Generator

## Target Buyers

### Primary
- **Small business owners (1–50 employees)** — first-time handbook, no HR department
- **HR managers at growth-stage companies (50–200 employees)** — updating outdated policies, formalizing culture
- **Startup founders** — scaling past 10 employees, investors asking for HR documentation

### Secondary
- **HR consultants** — use as a deliverable tool, bill clients $500–$1,500 for "custom handbook"
- **Agencies** — HR tech agencies, PEO brokers, employment law firms as a client resource
- **Bookkeepers/accountants** — often asked by small business clients "do I need a handbook?"

## Marketplace Listing Copy

**Title:** Employee Handbook & Company Policy Generator

**Tagline:** Stop paying lawyers $3K for a handbook. Get yours in 20 minutes.

**Short Description (150 words):**
Every business with employees needs a handbook — but most small business owners skip it (liability), grab generic templates (wrong), or pay a lawyer (overkill). This skill generates a complete, customized employee handbook in four prompts: core policies (code of conduct, PTO, remote work), compensation and benefits, performance and discipline, and legal compliance. Each section is tailored to your company name, state, industry, and culture — not a fill-in-the-blank template. Includes manager guidance notes so you can actually enforce what you write. Employment lawyers charge $2,000–$5,000 for this. HR consultants charge $150–$300/hour. You get it for $29, once, forever.

| Competitor | Price | Weakness |

| Employment lawyer | $2,000–$5,000 | Overkill for < 50 employees |

| Trainual | $249–$599/mo | Process docs, not policies |
| Generic ChatGPT | Free | No compliance awareness, no structure |

**Our edge:** Only option that is (1) customized to their company, (2) compliance-aware, (3) includes manager guidance, (4) at a one-time price under $30.
